A paragraph is a group of sentences that are organized around a single topic or idea. Paragraphs are a conventional way to structure written work, and they help readers follow the author's line of thought. Parts of a paragraphTopic sentence: The sentence that expresses the main idea of the paragraph Body sentences: Sentences that provide details, examples, or context to support the topic sentence Concluding sentence: The sentence that wraps up the paragraph Purpose of paragraphs Help readers understand the structure of the writingHelp readers understand the main points of the writingHelp readers understand how the ideas are connectedTips for writing paragraphsMake sure each paragraph has a clear topic and that the sentences support that topic Use a paragraph break to transition between ideas Include enough detail to fully develop the topic
